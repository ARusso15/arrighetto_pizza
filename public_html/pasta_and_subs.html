Pasta and Subs


Our pasta and subs our the greatest in all of New Jersey! Our pasta is made with hand made noodles and our homemade sauce made with our families secret recipe, using the finest Florida tomatoes.

Each pasta dish includes one of our specialty sandwiches. Made with real meat from all over the United States and our fresh artisan breads using wheat from Kansas, our sandwiches are packed with protein and carbs! Choose from either a traditional sub-style sandwich, or a stromboli.
